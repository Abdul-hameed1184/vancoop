How it works

From setup to daily operations in a clear digital flow. VantageCoops is designed to help cooperatives move faster without losing control, accountability, or approval structure.

1

Set up your organization

Create your cooperative profile, configure basic settings, assign administrators, and define how your organization operates.

2

Activate your modules

Enable the modules you need — special investment, target savings, contributions, wallet system, airtime and data cashbacks, marketplace, loans, reports, roles, and more.

3

Onboard members and vendors

Invite users into dedicated portals where they can complete actions without unnecessary back-and-forth.

4

Track everything in real time
